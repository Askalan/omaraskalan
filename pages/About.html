Who is Omar Askalan?

Omar Askalan is an agricultural engineer and irrigation (Ph.D.). He is an expert in irrigation design, Geographic Information System (GIS), data analysis, data science, automation irrigation, greenhouse structures, water safety, Somali farming investment, consulting services, in the areas of Agriculture Water Management, Irrigation Development and Management, climate change and who capable to develop a comprehensive insight to plan, prepare, manage and operate water, Irrigation and Environmental projects at different scales (small, medium and large at national, regional and International level).
